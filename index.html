Lip Oils

If you have been looking for a light-weight, non-sticky, moisturizing lip oil, look no further! These two lip oils are truly the best on the market. They have the perfect your lips but better effect to them. Ten out of ten recommend!

Ulta Beauty Juice Infused lip oil in 'Cranberry + Pomergranate'

Experience luscious lips like never before with Ulta Beauty's Juice Infused Lip Oil. This lip oil is top-tier if you want the ultimate shiny, moisturized lips that last all day long. The delicious scents and flavors that make every application a treat! My personal favorite 'Canberry + Pomegranate' gives your lips a naturally pretty pink tint! An honorable mention for the 'Pineapple' shade that has a gorgeous yellow tint.

NYX Fat Oil Lip Drip lip oil in 'My Main'

NYX's Fat Oil Lip Drip—a must-have lip oil for those who crave shine without the stickiness! It keeps your lips glistening for hours on end with it's amazing formula. If you're all about the clear lip gloss vibe for that ultimate shine, look no further than the shade 'My Main'. This clear lip oil offers everything you love about a good clear lip gloss, minus the weight and gloopy texture!

Lip Glosses

Nothing is worse than a sticky, thick lip gloss! The NYX Butter Glosses has never let me down!

NYX Butter Gloss lip gloss in 'Praline'

NYX Butter Glosses redefine the notion of lip gloss being thick with their ultra-thin, mega-shiny formula! Packed with pigment and a delightful scent, they're a definitely a treat. Thanks to their lightweight consistency, you'll forget you're even wearing lip gloss—it's that comfortable! The shade 'Praline' is a stunning brown nude that flatters every skin tone effortlessly. With an impressive lineup of 32 shades, NYX has something for everyone in their iconic butter gloss collection. And the best part? Despite their stellar performance, these glosses come at an incredibly affordable price point. Honorable mentions to the shades 'Creme Brulee' and 'Madeleine' as well.

Lip Plumpers

Nobody likes a lip plumper that doesn't work! But these two lip plumpers will get the job done every time!

Fenty Beauty Gloss Bomb Heat lip plumper in 'Fu$$y'

When it comes to hitting the mark, Fenty Beauty always delivers, and in my opinion, their lip products reign supreme. I've yet to encounter a Fenty Beauty lip product that didn't impress me. Among their stellar lineup, my absolute favorite has to be their Gloss Bomb Heat lip glosses. And let me tell you, they weren't kidding about the heat! These lip plumpers not only offer a stunning array of shades but also deliver on their promise to plump your lips. 'Fu$$y' stands out as the ideal pink nude shade that's simply divine.

Elf Lip Plumping lip gloss in 'Pink Paloma'

Meet your new everyday essential: Elf Lip Plumping Gloss, a staple in my routine for its sheer perfection! 'Pink Paloma' offers just the right hint of pink, making it ideal for daily wear or as a finishing touch to your lip combos. Unlike other plumpers, this gloss is anything but sticky, and while it may not pack as much of a burn, rest assured it still delivers those plump, juicy lips you want.

Ranking

Now for the ranking!

Narrowing down these top-tier lip products into a ranked list from 1 to 5 was no simple task, given that they're all cherished staples in my beauty routine. Each week, I find myself reaching for them interchangeably, making it challenging to choose favorites. Nevertheless, if pressed to assign a hierarchy, here's the order I'd settle on!

1st place is Elf's Lip Plumping Gloss! If you can only get one lip product from this list it should be this lip plumper because of how versatile it is.

2nd place is NYX's Fat Oil Lip Drip lip oil because how shinny it leaves your lips!

3rd place is Fenty Beauty's Gloss Bomb Heat for how plump and pink 'Fu$$y' will make your lips look!

4th is Ulta Beauty's Juice Infused lip oil that's great for every day where!

Last but not least is NYX's Butter Gloss is a staple everyone should have in their makeup bag!
